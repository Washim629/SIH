About CGC Alumni Association

The Chandigarh Group of Colleges (CGC) Alumni Association is a vibrant community of graduates who are committed to maintaining strong connections with their alma mater and fellow alumni. Our association serves as a bridge between the past and the present, fostering relationships that last a lifetime.

Founded in 2005, the CGC Alumni Association has grown into a powerful network of professionals spanning various industries across the globe. We strive to create opportunities for our members to engage, learn, and give back to the institution that has played a crucial role in shaping their careers.

Our Mission

To foster a lifelong connection between alumni and the college, promote professional growth, and support the development of current students and the institution.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and influential alumni network that contributes significantly to the growth and prestige of Chandigarh Group of Colleges and its graduates.
